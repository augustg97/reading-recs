About this place

Welcome, readers. This is a reading room for two — August and Julia — modeled on Read Something Wonderful.

Every pick here was chosen to offer something to each of you: essays, stories, long-form journalism, weird think pieces, and the occasional philosophical rabbit hole. Some lean toward one of you; all are meant to be worth the other's while. The center of gravity is the territory you share — minds and machines, institutions and their absurdities, elegant dread, and writing good enough to justify itself.
